MARCH: Fire Prevention Month
For the health and safety of every person, it is important to take the proper measures of learning how to prevent harmful fires and how to stay safe during one. In the Philippines, March is a month dedicated to this practice and we encourage others to stay aware and educated about it.


Avoiding Fires at Home
Never keep flammable items right next to a heat source and store them correctly in the proper places. Keep your attention on open flames and do not leave your kitchen unattended while in use.
Keep stoves, ovens, and such under regular maintenance. Turn off heat sources and unplug items in the house when not in use and when going outside.
If possible, install smoke alarms and make sure they work properly. Make sure to put out any fires properly. Do not rush into every fire with water as this may not always get rid of it.
